First Nation Land Loss Examples

Quebec and Nova Scotia

Goal

This section will examine potential land loss for First Nations Reserves. There are 8 reserves in 5 of the 10 provinces that are currently in litigation to be turned into National Parks, which means the Aboriginal peoples would lose that land. These visualizations will compare the current in-litigation reserve land for 2 provinces (Quebec and Nova Scotia) with the current area of First Nations communities in these provinces.
